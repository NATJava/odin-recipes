Pasta Recipe

Description:

Here is my favorite pasta recipe.

This recipe is so delicious and everyone should try it!

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces dry pasta
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.

  2. Step 2:

    Add pasta and cook for 5 to 7 minutes or until still slightly less than al dente; drain. Rinse with cold water.

  3. Step 3:

    Toss with olive oil.

  4. Step 4:

    Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.

  5. Step 5:

    To reheat, bring a large pot of water to a boil, add pasta and cook until hot, 1 to 2 minutes.

  6. Step 6:

    Enjoy!
